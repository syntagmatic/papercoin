PaperCoin

Bitcoin in an origami wallet.

What is PaperCoin?

PaperCoin is a Bitcoin paper wallet generator.

Each PaperCoin consists of a Bitcoin address and private key to access the bitcoin at that address. When folded, the private key is safely hidden inside the PaperCoin. The address and key are represented as both an alphanumeric string and QR code.

Keys are randomly generated in your browser, not on our server. This is the only safe way to generate key pairs on a website. For extra security, follow the precautions in the producing safe paper wallets guide.

Creating PaperCoins

Print!
  1. Click the print button →
  2. Print the page
  3. Follow the instructions on the paper
  4. Send coins to the wallet
  5. Enjoy!

Redeeming PaperCoins

To access funds on a Papercoin, unfold it and import the private key into your wallet. Follow the instructions for the wallet you use:

  • Electrum
    1. Import Menu → Private Keys
    2. Enter the alphanumeric private key
    3. Import
    4. Transfer the bitcoin from the imported address to an address you control
    5. Remember, imported addresses are not recoverable from the 12-word mnemonic backup!
  • Bitcoin-Qt Instructions
  • Multibit Instructions
  • Armory Instructions
  • Blockchain.info Android App Instructions

Once you've imported the private key, send the bitcoin to an address you control. Whoever printed your PaperCoin may have kept a copy of the private key, so both of you will have access to the bitcoin until you transfer it.
