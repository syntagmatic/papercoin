What is PaperCoin?

PaperCoin is a Bitcoin paper wallet generator.

Each PaperCoin consists of a Bitcoin address and private key to access the bitcoin at that address. When folded, the private key is safely hidden inside the PaperCoin. The address and key are represented as both an alphanumeric string and QR code.

Keys are randomly generated in your browser, not on our server. This is the only safe way to generate key pairs on a website. For extra security, follow the precautions in the producing safe paper wallets guide.
